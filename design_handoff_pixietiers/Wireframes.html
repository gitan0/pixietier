pixietiers · wireframes v1

What this is

Low-fi wireframes exploring 3 structural directions for the tier-list editor, share page, and stats dashboard. Final direction is dark, cinematic, premium-gaming, inspired by the pixiechess.xyz dashboard — these are grayscale on purpose so we're arguing about structure.

How to read

Circles = chess piece thumbnails. orange pill = design intention note. Dashed boxes = placeholder / upload zones. Use the tabs above to switch direction; Editor / Share toggle on the right.

Next steps

Pick a direction (or a mix). I'll render one as a mid-fi mock in the actual dark cinematic palette, with real chess piece artwork placeholders and motion notes for drag interactions.
